## New projects (didactic blurbs)

**02.06 — GPU ICP** (★ beginner, domain 02, flagship). Teaches scan registration from physics up:
what a LiDAR return is (and why σ = 5 mm), the ICP problem, the small-angle SE(3) Jacobians for
both cost functions, and the *sliding degrees of freedom* argument for why plane constraints
dominate on structured scenes — then proves it with the convergence curves in
`demo/out/convergence.csv`. GPU lessons: brute-force NN as the honest teaching baseline (KD-trees
are 02.05), and the block-level reduction that turns 30,000 per-point Jacobian contributions into
one 6×6 system. The single most interesting thing to look at: the two convergence curves plotted
together — 48 iterations vs 6.
